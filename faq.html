How I Work

As an evidential medium, I am able to receive messages from the spirit world — and provide evidence allowing you to identify the communicator. I receive my messages by seeing, hearing, feeling, smelling, and knowing. Sometimes, I'll even taste something that evokes a memory for the client.

For example, I might say: “I know I am with a man who feels like a father figure. I see him driving a red pickup, and I can feel it rolling over. I see a pack of cigarettes in his shirt pocket, and I can hear him coughing.” And then, I'll stop to check in with you to confirm that this is indeed your father, that he had a pickup and experienced an accident with you as a passenger, that he smoked and died of related causes. After we've confirmed the details, we'll proceed with the reading, during which the spirit will provide more evidence and a message for you.

As a Certified Grief Educator, I have been trained to approach every session using trauma-informed practices. The goal of each session is to offer validation, comfort, and peace — knowing that love and life continue even when our physical bodies no longer exist.

I work strictly with the Light, and am inspired to bring peace and love to all who want to receive a reading, both in this world and the next.

Common questions

Should I tell you about the person I want to hear from beforehand?

No — please don't. For the most authentic experience, I ask that you do not share any personal information or details about yourself or the person you wish to connect with beforehand. Evidence of Spirit's presence and communication will arise naturally during the sitting, directly from those in the next world.

Can you guarantee that a specific loved one will come through?

I cannot. I don't ask for the name of the person you hope to hear from, and I cannot guarantee a specific loved one will step forward. The spirit people who do come always come for your highest and best good.

How should I respond during the reading?

During the evidential portion of the reading, please respond only with “Yes”, “No”, “I don't know”, “I'm not sure”, or “Need more info”. This keeps the evidence clean and ensures it's truly coming from Spirit and not from me reading you.

Will I have time to ask questions?

Yes. Each reading reserves time at the end for feedback and any questions you'd like to bring forward.

Should I take notes?

I recommend you take notes or audio-record the reading, because some details may not make immediate sense and become clear later, sometimes days or weeks afterward.

Is this a psychic reading?

No. This is not a psychic reading. Mediumship focuses on connecting with loved ones in Spirit rather than predicting future events or giving life advice. Every session unfolds naturally according to the needs of Spirit and the sitter.

How are sessions held?

All sessions are held remotely — over Zoom or by phone. Sessions are 30 or 60 minutes. See Services & Pricing for details.

What if I need to reschedule or cancel?

You can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment for a full refund. See the full cancellation policy on the Services page.
