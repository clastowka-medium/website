About Carol

A lifetime of experiences with Spirit

I was raised with stories of relatives coming back to visit after they'd died: my uncle's rocking chair that moved by itself, my aunt's uneven footsteps in the hallway, a ball of blue light rising out of my great aunt's body, circling the room, then disappearing at the moment she passed.

Years later, after my beloved husband Greg crossed over, the messages started pouring in. My dream is to make that connection possible for everyone who wants to hear from those they've lost.

Thank you for reaching out! I'm Carol, an Evidential Medium mentored by internationally renowned medium Joseph Shiel. This is not a psychic reading, but an evidential connection with Spirit.

In our 30-minute or 60-minute live Zoom session, I bring through clear details to help you recognize your spirit communicator/s. I will tell you exactly what I see, feel, hear, or experience in order to allow the spirit to provide their own identifying evidence.

I do not ask for the name of the person you hope to hear from. I also cannot guarantee a specific loved one will come through. Spirit people who do step forward always come for your highest and best good. I recommend you take notes or audio-record the reading, because some details may make sense later.

I will always treat you and those in spirit with compassion and respect. Messages are supportive and positive. During the evidential portion, please respond only with "Yes", "No", "I don't know", or "I'm not sure" or "Need more info".

Each reading reserves time at the end for feedback. I can't wait to work with you!
